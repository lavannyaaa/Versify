Still I Rise

I might not be successful
I might not be at my highest
But am neither at my lowest
I know I have achieved a lot
I know I have improved a lot
Yet I still have much more to climb
Yet I need to achieve more
But I trust myself that I can
I trust myself that someday I will
I trust myself that I will be the better
And will always try to be the best version of myself
Untill then I'll focus on my goals
I'll try to do my best
I'll fall , I'll climb , I'll do everything
Until I come to the realization that
I have accomplished my desired goals
