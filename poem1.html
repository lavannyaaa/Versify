Unrequited Love

In the depth of Unrequited endearment ,
With hopes and longings and cravings ,
I find myself dwell in realm of one sided love ,
In the nostalgia that's exclusively mine.

I watch from afar, hidden in shadows ,
Admiring your existence from a distance ,
Like a sunflower trying to reach the sun ,
Unseen , unheard and yet lingering in depth of consciousness.

For the every smile you show the world ,
For the every twinkle in your eyes ,
Each moment it aches deeper ,
As I cherish your presence every now and then.

I know that you don't belong to me ,
I have realization that I can never have you
Yet I Envision your presence on canvas of my dreams ,
embracing the hope that our paths may one day intertwine.

Until then, I'll carry this unrequited love,
With grace, with strength, with a gentle sigh,
Waiting for a magical moment to happen ,
In the fictional world where you're mine.
