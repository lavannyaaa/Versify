Masked Agony

Yes I dwell in a world full of people ,
Yes I sail in the same boat as others ,
Yes I am a part of all the hustle ,
Yes I am surrounded by mankind ,
But when I close my eyes ,
I find myself alone in a vast and empty room ,
With all the emotions hidden in my heart ,
With all the sorrow emptiness and solitude ,
With all the feelings I hide from the world ,
I find myself suffering alone ,
Where no one is mine ,
Where no one is trying to reach me ,
Where no one cares about my crumbles ,
I find myself broken and suffering ,
Yet I carry the guts to smile ,
Yet I show the world I am fine ,
I show the world a face which is not actually mine.
